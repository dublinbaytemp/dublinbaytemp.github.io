An unofficial community service for Dublin Bay swimmers. Readings come from the Dublin Bay buoy at the Safe Water Mark (Commissioners of Irish Lights), via the community API at dublinbaybuoy.com. Tide predictions: Marine Institute. Bathing water quality & swim notices: EPA. Air temperature: Met Éireann observations (Dublin Airport).

Data can lag or misbehave — always judge conditions yourself before getting in.
