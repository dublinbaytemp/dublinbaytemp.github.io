Readings from the Dublin Bay buoy at the Safe Water Mark, via the Commissioners of Irish Lights' public data archive. The archive begins in February 2014; 2015 is excluded (its sensor was unreliable all year), implausible values (outside 2–25°C) are rejected, and a reading must sit within 1.5°C of its neighbours to count.

Live conditions: dublinbaytemp.github.io · updated twice daily on X and Bluesky.
